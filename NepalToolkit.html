About Bikram Sambat

Bikram Sambat (BS) is Nepal's official calendar, running approximately 56 years and 8.5 months ahead of the Gregorian calendar.

The BS calendar has 12 months ranging from 29 to 32 days, with no fixed length per month — each year's month lengths are determined by astronomical calculations. This converter supports dates from 1975 BS to 2099 BS.
